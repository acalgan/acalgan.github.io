About

I obtained by BSc. degree at Izmir Institute of Technology, Computer Engineering Department. Currently, I am working as a software engineer at ASELSAN

I have graduated from Cognitive Science master's programme at Informatics Institute, METU During my master's studies, I focus on solving NLP tasks using Neural Networks. For my thesis, I have built alternative Neural Networks to model the Turkish language. I design both LSTM and GRU networks as well as stacked LSTMs and GRUs by using Tensorflow. The main motivation of my thesis was to explore the correlation between my language model predictions and human predictions on word level.
